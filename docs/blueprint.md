# **App Name**: EASY Prep Master

## Core Features:

- Gamified Training Modules: Engage in timed sections featuring reading comprehension, verbal analogies, and error detection with an energetic UI.
- AI Verbal Practice Tool: An AI powered engine that generates unique verbal analogy questions and provides semantic explanations to clarify logical relationships.
- Intelligent Review Log: Track performance and persist a dynamic history of mistakes to provide focused study sessions on weak areas.
- Countdown Pressure Engine: Adaptive countdown timers that sync with session performance to simulate high-stakes testing environments.
- Personal Question Vault: Mark and organize favorite or complex questions for later review with custom categorization.
- Global Performance Analytics: A dashboard visualizing score progression and comparative rankings using interactive charts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Goldenrod (#E6AC00), evoking focus and achievement against dark themes.
- Background: Midnight Ember (#1A1712), a heavily desaturated dark base with warm undertones to minimize eye strain during study.
- Accent color: Neon Vermillion (#FF4D33), providing high contrast for active timers and critical alerts.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' for a precise, techy, and structured academic feel.
- Body font: 'Inter' for clean readability across long reading passages and multi-choice options.
- Spacious card-based layout with thick glassmorphic borders and deep shadows to prioritize focus.
- Subtle pulse and glow transitions on correct answers and progress bars to create a satisfying gamified feedback loop.
